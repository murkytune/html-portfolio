The Best Movies According to Mark

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Star Wars

Breaking new ground in special effects and taking imagination to new places, Star Wars opened up minds and completely changed cinema ushing in a new genre.

The Matrix

Special effects is taken to a new level with this mind-bending action fantasy. Multiple levels of philosophy are explored and woven into a thrilling epic of the hero's journey.

The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers

The second offering into the greatest trilogy of all time. J.R.R. Tolkien's classic books are turned into an amazing cinematic achievement.
